Santaphobia

For the Wikibook project, our objective was to select a specific topic, curate its content from Wikipedia, and create a comprehensive book. I decided to focus on Santa Claus and its counterpart, Saint Nicholas, aiming to explore ways in which a figure's reputation can be influenced by its historical events, cultures and commodity. The design process of this book involves a meticulous assessment of the gathered content, followed by the creation of a well-suited grid to organize the information effectively. Additionally, I conducted experiments with various typefaces and layout designs to enhance the overall aesthetic appeal and readability of the book.
