21st Century Conversation Guide:
Modes of Communication: Speaking & Typing

21st Century Conversation Guide is an exploration of innovative conversation experiences that can be facilitated through different communication methods, with or without the element of time introduced by texting. By presenting complete dialogues and incorporating feedback from participants, the project provides real-life examples and insights into how different approaches can be employed to address specific conversational challenges. The step-by-step guidance included in the book empowers readers to proactively tackle communication obstacles and adapt their conversational style to different contexts. Ultimately, the project aims to venture readers into trying new conversation modes for themselves, exploring alternative ways of engaging with others as well as problem-solving through communication.
